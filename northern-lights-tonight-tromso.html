Will you see the aurora in Tromsø tonight?

Three things have to line up: darkness, clear skies, and geomagnetic activity. Tromsø sits directly under the auroral oval, so activity is rarely the problem — the Northern Lights can appear here even at a low KP index. The real gamble is the weather: it is cloudy in Tromsø a large share of winter nights, and a clear-sky window can open and close within an hour. That is exactly why a live camera beats a forecast — it sees the clouds a KP number can't.

Best hours

Roughly 6 PM to 1 AM local time, peaking around 10 PM–midnight. The sky must be dark.

Season

Late September to late March. In summer the midnight sun makes the aurora invisible.

KP index

In Tromsø the aurora can show at KP 1–2. Higher KP means brighter, more overhead displays.
